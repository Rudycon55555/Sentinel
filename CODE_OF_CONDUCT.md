# Sentinel Code of Conduct

Sentinel is an open, collaborative project.  
To maintain a welcoming and productive environment, all contributors and users are expected to follow this Code of Conduct.

## Our Standards

We commit to:

- Being respectful and inclusive  
- Welcoming diverse viewpoints  
- Providing constructive feedback  
- Supporting beginners and experts alike  
- Maintaining a safe, harassmentâ€‘free environment  

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks  
- Hate speech or derogatory comments  
- Threats or intimidation  
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful behavior  
- Sharing private information without consent  

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards  
- Enforcing the Code of Conduct  
- Taking appropriate action when violations occur  

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warnings  
- Temporary or permanent bans  
- Reversal of contributions  
- Escalation to platform administrators
